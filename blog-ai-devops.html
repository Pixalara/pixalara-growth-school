Will AI Replace DevOps Engineer Roles?

With the rise of ChatGPT, Claude, and GitHub Copilot, many students are worried. "Why would a company hire me if AI can write the code?"

The Reality Check

AI is good at writing snippets of code. It is bad at understanding context. AI cannot join a meeting with a client, understand their business pain, and design a custom architecture to solve it.

How AI changes the job

Instead of typing boring YAML configuration files for 3 hours, you will ask AI to generate the draft in 3 seconds. Then, you will spend your time optimizing security and cost.

"AI will not replace DevOps engineers. DevOps engineers who use AI will replace those who don't."
